About the Project

Project Overview

The Heart Disease Analysis project aims to analyze patient health data to identify risk factors associated with heart disease. Through interactive dashboards and visual analytics, the project helps healthcare professionals understand trends, support decision-making, and promote early detection of heart-related conditions.
